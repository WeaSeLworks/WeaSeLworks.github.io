Manage livestock remotely with smart tags

Check the status of your livestock from your smart phone

Livestock Management

Update records from anywhere on your smart phone, with or without signal. Then sync them to the cloud.

Health insights

Early lameness detection enables focus on the animals that need attention. Easy bulk/individual recording of disease & drugs administered.

Security

Get notified when a flock exhibits behaviour as group. Get instant alerts when dogs attack livestock. Early warning system for animal theft.

Early detection is
essential

Diseases such Scald, Footrot and CODD cost UK sheep farmers Â£90m in 2017. Farm Hand can protect flock health by identifying lameness early.

Movement and temperature sensors are used to track and report behaviour in the field. The system detects abnormal behaviour in individual animals allowing early intervention.

Unlock the power of your data

The software will act as your flock record, it can be updated in the field, manage 1000's of sheep in a few taps. No need for pen or paper, our smart tags are location aware making updating your flock records easy.

Simple to set up alerts to suit your farm and flock. Unlock the power of your data to increase animal health and profit.
